This project can interoperate with external MathType / MTEF conversion
projects when users explicitly install and enable those tools.

External projects referenced by the public pipeline:

- transpect/mathtype-extension
- jure/mathtype_to_mathml
- jure/mathtype, as incorporated by transpect/mathtype-extension

The full external repositories are not vendored in this repository.
Users who install or vendor them must follow those projects' licenses
and retain their notices.
